# Alpha 0.2.0 — Obtención de Cartas

## Intent

Definir cómo los Cantores obtienen cartas de Fundamentos y Rudimentos durante la campaña usando Auralita Refinada, de forma que adquirir habilidades compita con mejorar el Instrumento Vital y vuelva la Auralita Refinada una decisión estratégica de grupo.

## Changes

- Added `instrument/obtencion-de-cartas.md` as a canonical rules module.
- Established that each Cantor starts with 2 Rudiments and 0 Fundamentos.
- Defined that initial Rudiments are normally chosen by the player, unless the DM restricts or assigns them for campaign reasons.
- Added the requirement that a Cantor's Instrumento Vital must have rank D or higher in every attribute of a card's Afinación to acquire it.
- Added Auralita Refinada costs for simple and combined Rudiments and Fundamentos.
- Clarified that Auralita Refinada can be shared to pay for a card even if the acquired card belongs to one Cantor.
- Added two optional purchase variants: classic purchase and random purchase/sobres, with no default variant.
- Defined that random sobres do not contain duplicates and that cards from sobres are assigned once to a Cantor and cannot be transferred again.
- Added the new module to the manifest and updated Instrumento Vital section numbering.

## Design Notes

The approved costs keep ordinary cards cheaper than major Instrumento Vital improvements while still making them compete for the same shared resource. Fundamentos cost slightly more than Rudiments because they are passive and meant to feel more special. Combined cards require rank D or higher in both attributes and cost more than single-attribute cards.
