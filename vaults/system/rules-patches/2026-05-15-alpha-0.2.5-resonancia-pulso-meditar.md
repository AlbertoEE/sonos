# Alpha 0.2.5 — Resonancia por Pulso y Meditar

## Intent

Dejar claro que un Pulso siempre recupera 1 de Resonancia aunque falle, y simplificar Meditar en combate para que recupere 1d4 de Resonancia en vez de depender de 1dAT.

## Changes

- Clarified that any Pulso recovers 1 Resonancia whether it hits or misses.
- Changed Meditar from recovering 1dAT Resonancia to recovering 1d4 Resonancia.
- Added that Resonancia recovered cannot exceed the Cantor's maximum Resonancia.
- Clarified that Amortiguado predominates over Resonancia recovery, including Pulso recovery.
- Marked RT-005 as resolved.

## Design Notes

This keeps Pulso as the reliable baseline generator for Resonancia while preventing Meditar from scaling dynamically. Amortiguado remains the explicit exception that blocks all Resonancia recovery.
