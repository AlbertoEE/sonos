# Alpha 0.2.2 — Costes de Progreso del Instrumento Vital

## Intent

Añadir costes objetivo de referencia para que el DM pueda usar la fórmula de Auralita Refinada por sesión con valores concretos según cuánto desarrollo del Instrumento Vital quiera permitir durante la campaña.

## Changes

- Added a `Costes de referencia de progreso` section to `Mejoras del Instrumento Vital`.
- Added the natural full development reference cost: 134 Auralita Refinada, approximately 27 sessions at 5 Auralita per Cantor per session.
- Added the extreme corrupt development reference cost: 334+ Auralita Refinada, approximately 67+ sessions at 5 Auralita per Cantor per session.
- Clarified that these costs assume an initial Instrumento Vital with one attribute at C.
- Clarified that the reference costs do not include card purchases, Purificación de Desafinación, recovered Auralita, failed improvements, or extra costs from corrupt improvement consequences.
- Clarified that corrupt progression can exceed closed natural limits if the DM allows repeated forbidden improvements beyond the reference point.

## Design Notes

The natural value uses the existing improvement table as the ordinary limit: base improvement purchases, four Resonancia increases, one Despertar Armónico, and two attributes raised to S. The corrupt reference extends that baseline by repeating Despertar Armónico through Mejoras Corruptas to bring the remaining four attributes to S.
